# Momentum Wellness First-Week Launch Plan

This is a simple launch plan for the first week Momentum Wellness is actively
shared. Start with warm contacts and manual follow-up before adding paid ads,
large outreach lists, or automated systems.

## Launch Goal

Validate that the website, contact form, Trainerize enrollment path, and manual
follow-up process work smoothly with real people.

## Recommended Audience

Start with warm contacts first:

- People who already know Momentum Wellness or the owner
- Friends, family, colleagues, and local connections who may know someone
  looking for app-based coaching
- Business owners, HR contacts, managers, or community contacts who may be
  open to employee wellness conversations

Avoid cold mass outreach until the lead response, tracker, and onboarding flow
feel smooth.

## Day 1: Final Readiness Check

1. Open the public website.
2. Check the homepage, Programs, Pricing, Contact, and Employee Wellness pages.
3. Submit one contact-form test with a real email.
4. Confirm the notification reaches the business email.
5. Confirm the Trainerize enrollment link opens correctly.
6. Confirm the admin tracker is available.
7. Delete any test tracker records.

## Day 2: Soft Announcement

1. Post the short launch message on personal or business social channels.
2. Send the business email announcement to a small warm list.
3. Record any replies that need follow-up.
4. Create tracker records only for real leads or meaningful follow-up.

## Day 3: Individual Coaching Outreach

1. Send a warm individual outreach message to people who may be interested or
   may know someone who is.
2. Direct them to the Programs page or Trainerize enrollment page.
3. Use the individual lead reply template for questions.
4. Track only real follow-up needs.

## Day 4: Corporate Warm Outreach

1. Send the corporate warm outreach message to a small list of business or HR
   contacts.
2. Direct them to the Employee Wellness Programs page.
3. Use the corporate lead reply template for responses.
4. Record company leads in the onboarding tracker.

## Day 5: Follow-Up And Review

1. Follow up with anyone who replied but has not taken the next step.
2. Review Formspree submissions.
3. Review tracker records.
4. Confirm there are no broken links or form issues.
5. Note common questions for future FAQ or website improvements.

## Weekend Or End-Of-Week Review

Review:

- Number of website inquiries
- Number of individual coaching conversations
- Number of Trainerize enrollment clicks or purchases if available
- Number of corporate conversations
- Questions people asked repeatedly
- Any friction in the onboarding process
- Any website copy that confused people

## Do Not Do Yet

- Do not launch paid ads yet.
- Do not send mass cold emails yet.
- Do not automate onboarding actions yet.
- Do not publish corporate pricing.
- Do not collect sensitive health information through email or the tracker.
- Do not promise specific health, business, or financial outcomes.

## Success For Week One

Week one is successful if:

- The website works.
- The contact form works.
- Leads can be replied to manually.
- The tracker helps organize follow-up.
- The Trainerize path works for an individual client.
- Momentum Wellness learns what real people ask before scaling outreach.
