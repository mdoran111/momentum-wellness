# Momentum Wellness Online Training Marketing Plan

This plan focuses on attracting individual clients for online app-based
training. It should be used before paid advertising or complex automation.

Do not make guaranteed weight-loss claims, medical claims, fake testimonials,
healthcare-cost savings promises, or unsupported client-result claims.

## Primary Offer

Adult Strength is the current individual online training offer.

Position it as:

- App-based fitness and nutrition structure
- Habit tracking
- Progress tools
- Accountability
- Monthly programming
- Clear support for people who need consistency

Keep the message simple:

Momentum Wellness helps people follow a clearer plan, stay accountable, and
build better health habits through a modern app-based coaching system.

## Ideal Online Client

Start with people who:

- Want structure but do not want to figure everything out alone
- Need accountability
- Want strength training with practical nutrition habits
- Are beginners or intermediate lifters
- Have busy schedules
- Want an app-based program they can follow at home or in a gym
- Are tired of starting over without a system

Do not position the program as medical care, therapy, physical therapy, or a
guaranteed transformation program.

## Conversion Flow

Simple early-stage flow:

1. Social post, message, or conversation introduces Momentum Wellness.
2. Person visits the Programs page.
3. Person clicks **Start Your Program**.
4. Person enrolls through Trainerize.
5. Trainerize and Stripe handle purchase.
6. Momentum Wellness manually verifies onboarding.
7. Client follows the app-based program.

For questions:

1. Person uses the Contact page.
2. Formspree sends the inquiry to business email.
3. Momentum Wellness replies manually.
4. Real follow-up is tracked in the onboarding tracker.

## Short Video Script Templates

### What Adult Strength Is

Adult Strength is an app-based Momentum Wellness program built for people who
want structure, accountability, and a clearer fitness plan. Inside the app, you
can follow training, track habits, review progress, and stay connected to the
system. The goal is not perfection. The goal is consistency.

### Why Structure Matters

Most people do not need more random workouts. They need a plan they can return
to. Momentum Wellness gives you training structure, habit tracking, and
accountability inside one app so you know what to do next.

### Missed Workout Reset

Missing a workout does not mean the week is ruined. Open your calendar, look at
the next realistic action, and continue. Momentum is built by returning to the
plan.

### App Walkthrough

Inside the Momentum Wellness app, clients can see their workouts, habits,
progress tools, and program structure in one place. That keeps the process
clear and easier to follow.

## Weekly Marketing Checklist

Do this once per week:

1. Publish three to five pieces of content.
2. Send a small number of warm outreach messages.
3. Reply to every real inquiry.
4. Add real leads to the onboarding tracker.
5. Review which posts or messages created conversations.
6. Note common questions for future content.
7. Keep the Programs page and Trainerize link as the main conversion path.

## Metrics To Track Manually

Start simple:

- Website contact inquiries
- Trainerize purchases
- Warm outreach replies
- Social post conversations
- Questions asked repeatedly
- Leads who need follow-up
- Leads who joined
- Leads who declined or went quiet

Do not add complicated analytics until the manual flow is working.

## When To Add Automation

Add automation only after:

- Contact form delivery is reliable.
- Trainerize purchase/onboarding flow is verified.
- Manual lead replies are working.
- Tracker follow-up process is consistent.
- The business knows which marketing messages are creating real leads.

Safe first marketing automation:

- Draft email replies for review
- Remind Momentum Wellness to follow up
- Track campaign source manually or with simple UTM links
- Add analytics provider after privacy and consent needs are reviewed
