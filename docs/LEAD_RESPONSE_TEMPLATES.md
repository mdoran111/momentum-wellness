# Momentum Wellness Lead Response Templates

Use these templates for manual replies to website inquiries until a reviewed
email workflow is approved. Keep replies personal, practical, and concise.

Do not include medical advice, guaranteed results, corporate pricing, payment
details, or private client information in these emails.

## Usage Rules

- Reply from the approved Momentum Wellness business email.
- Respond within one business day whenever practical.
- Personalize the first name, inquiry details, and next step.
- Keep individual and corporate inquiries separate.
- Do not ask leads to send medical history, payment details, progress photos,
  food logs, or sensitive health information by email.
- Record the lead and next action in the shared onboarding tracker when
  follow-up is needed.

## Individual Lead Reply

**Use when:** Someone asks about individual coaching, app-based training,
pricing, or getting started.

**Subject:** Momentum Wellness individual coaching

Hi {firstName},

Thank you for reaching out to Momentum Wellness.

Our individual program is built for people who want more structure,
accountability, and support through the Momentum Wellness app. It includes
training structure, nutrition guidance, habit tracking, progress tools, and
accountability to help you stay consistent over time.

The next step is to review the program details and decide whether you are ready
to start. You can view the program here:

https://www.trainerize.me/profile/momentumwellness6/?planGUID=8bb77914b268416bac5814c814ea283d

If you have questions before joining, reply here and we will help point you in
the right direction.

Momentum Wellness

## Corporate Wellness Lead Reply

**Use when:** A company, HR leader, owner, or manager asks about employee
wellness programs.

**Subject:** Momentum Wellness employee wellness consultation

Hi {firstName},

Thank you for contacting Momentum Wellness about an employee wellness program
for {companyName}.

Momentum Wellness provides app-based employee wellness programs for companies
with 50 or more employees. The program is built around training, nutrition,
habits, accountability, and community, with a focus on workforce health,
energy, morale, engagement, and long-term behavior change.

The next step is a consultation so we can understand your team, goals, timing,
and desired level of support. Please send a few times that work for a
30-minute conversation, or reply with any additional context you would like us
to review beforehand.

You can also learn more here:

https://momentumwellness.health/employee-wellness-programs

Momentum Wellness

## General Inquiry Reply

**Use when:** The message is not clearly individual coaching or corporate
wellness yet.

**Subject:** Thanks for contacting Momentum Wellness

Hi {firstName},

Thank you for reaching out to Momentum Wellness.

We help individuals and companies build better health habits through app-based
training, nutrition structure, habit tracking, accountability, and community
support.

To make sure we point you in the right direction, could you reply with a little
more context about what you are looking for?

- Individual coaching through the Momentum Wellness app
- Employee wellness programs for a company
- General information or another question

Once we know the best fit, we will send the right next step.

Momentum Wellness

## Optional Follow-Up If No Reply

**Use when:** A lead has not replied after several business days.

**Subject:** Following up from Momentum Wellness

Hi {firstName},

I wanted to follow up on your Momentum Wellness inquiry.

If you are still interested, reply here and we can help with the next step. If
the timing is not right, no problem. You can reach back out whenever you are
ready.

Momentum Wellness
