# Adult Strength Onboarding Content

This file contains approved draft copy for the Momentum Wellness Adult Strength
client onboarding experience. Replace bracketed placeholders before publishing.

## Recommended Client Sequence

1. The client selects **Start Your Program** on the Momentum Wellness website
   and completes the Adult Strength purchase through Trainerize.
2. Trainerize's connected Stripe account confirms the $150 monthly purchase.
3. On the purchase date, Trainerize creates or updates the client as Full
   Access with one-way messaging and subscribes the client to Adult Strength.
4. Trainerize sends its standard invitation email.
5. Trainerize sends the custom Momentum Wellness welcome email below.
6. The client activates their account and watches the in-app welcome video.
7. The client completes the Exercise Readiness & Consultation Form.
8. The client signs the liability waiver through an approved e-signature tool.
9. A Momentum Wellness administrator reviews any readiness concerns before the
   client begins exercise.
10. The client sees the first-sign-in message and starts the assigned program.

This workflow does not require Zapier. Trainerize and its connected Stripe
account handle the current purchase and program-start actions. Use the manual
onboarding tracker only to confirm completion, handle exceptions, and record
human review decisions without storing sensitive health details.

## Welcome Video

Trainerize displays the Welcome Video separately from the welcome email. In a
Studio Plus custom branded app, it appears during the client's first app login.

### Recommended Recording Format

- Orientation: portrait
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920
- File type: MP4 or MOV
- Length: 60 to 90 seconds; Trainerize permits up to 2 minutes
- Setting: clean, quiet, well-lit space with the Momentum Wellness logo visible
- Delivery: calm, direct, professional, and encouraging
- Framing: camera at eye level, chest-up composition, uncluttered background

## Exercise Readiness & Consultation Questions

Use these questions to build the Trainerize E-Consultation Form. This is a
custom Momentum Wellness screening form, not the official PAR-Q+.

### Profile and Contact

1. **Preferred name** — Short answer — Required
2. **Best phone number** — Short answer — Required
3. **Emergency contact name** — Short answer — Required
4. **Emergency contact phone number** — Short answer — Required

### Goals and Experience

5. **What are your top one to three goals for joining Adult Strength?** —
   Long answer — Required
6. **What would meaningful progress look like to you over the next three to
   six months?** — Long answer — Required
7. **How many days per week can you realistically dedicate to training?** —
   Multiple choice: 2, 3, 4, 5 or more — Required
8. **How would you describe your current training experience?** — Multiple
   choice: New to strength training, Returning after a break, Consistent for
   less than one year, Consistent for one year or more — Required
9. **Where will you complete most workouts?** — Multiple choice: Commercial
   gym, Home gym, Limited home equipment, Other — Required
10. **What equipment do you expect to have available?** — Long answer —
    Required
11. **Are there exercises or types of training you especially enjoy or want to
    avoid?** — Long answer — Optional

### Exercise Readiness

12. **Has a physician or other qualified healthcare professional told you not
    to exercise, or to exercise only under medical supervision?** — Yes/No —
    Required
13. **Do you experience chest discomfort during physical activity or at
    rest?** — Yes/No — Required
14. **Have you recently experienced fainting, unexplained dizziness, loss of
    balance, or unusual shortness of breath?** — Yes/No — Required
15. **Do you have a current bone, joint, muscle, or movement concern that may
    be worsened by exercise?** — Yes/No — Required
16. **Are you taking medication, recovering from a procedure, pregnant,
    recently postpartum, or managing another circumstance that may require
    exercise modifications or medical clearance?** — Yes/No — Required
17. **Is there any other reason you believe you should speak with a qualified
    healthcare professional before increasing your physical activity?** —
    Yes/No — Required
18. **If you answered Yes to any readiness question, briefly describe only the
    exercise limitation or accommodation Momentum Wellness should know. Do not
    upload medical records or include unrelated medical details.** — Long
    answer — Conditional
19. **Have you received clearance to participate in an independent exercise
    program when clearance is appropriate?** — Multiple choice: Yes, No, Not
    applicable, I am unsure — Required

### Preferences and Acknowledgment

20. **What usually makes it difficult for you to stay consistent?** — Long
    answer — Required
21. **What type of accountability is most helpful to you?** — Multiple choice:
    Clear schedule, Progress tracking, Reminders, Educational guidance, Other —
    Required
22. **Anything else you want Momentum Wellness to know about your schedule,
    preferences, or training setup?** — Long answer — Optional
23. **I understand this form is a general exercise-readiness screen, not a
    medical evaluation or diagnosis. I will seek advice from a qualified
    healthcare professional when appropriate and will tell Momentum Wellness
    if my readiness to exercise changes.** — Required acknowledgment

## Review Rule

Do not automatically clear every client. If the client answers Yes to a
readiness question or selects No or I am unsure for medical clearance:

1. Mark the client record as **Needs Review**.
2. Do not send a message stating that the client is medically cleared.
3. Review the response before recommending exercise modifications.
4. Ask the client to consult a qualified healthcare professional when
   appropriate.
5. Record only the operational outcome, such as **Cleared to Begin**,
   **Modifications Required**, or **Waiting for Client**, rather than copying
   detailed medical information into spreadsheets or public project files.

## Trainerize Setup Locations

- **Custom welcome email:** Settings > Auto Messages and Events
- **First sign-in message:** Settings > Auto Messages and Events
- **E-Consultation Form:** Settings > Consultation Form
- **Welcome video:** Add-ons > Custom Branded Mobile App > Welcome Video

Trainerize can attach a PDF to the custom welcome email, but an attached waiver
is not the same as a signed waiver. Store each completed client waiver in that
client's Trainerize profile. The onboarding tracker should only record the
operational status, such as `Waiver received`, and should not contain signed
forms, signatures, or private client details.

## Confirmed Adult Strength Product Behavior

- Product: Adult Strength
- Price: $150 per month
- Payment processor: Stripe connected inside Trainerize
- Product start: day of purchase
- Access at start: Full Access with one-way messaging
- Program action at start: subscribe the client to Adult Strength
- Cancellation/end action: manually deactivate the client when full app access
  should end, and confirm the Adult Strength program is no longer available
- Zapier: not required for the current purchase and program-start workflow

Before relying on the cancellation path, test it with a non-client test account
and write down who is responsible for deactivation. Do not assume a cancelled
Stripe subscription automatically deactivates the Trainerize client unless the
full cancellation flow has been confirmed.
