# Individual Program Offer

## Offer Summary

Momentum Wellness provides app-based fitness, nutrition, habit,
accountability, and progress-tracking programs for individuals who want
structure, consistency, and support.

The current public offer is called **Complete Wellness Coaching** and is listed
at `$150 per month`, billed monthly, with cancellation available at any time.

Confirm pricing and terms before implementing payments or changing sales copy.

## Who It Is For

The offer is positioned for:

- Beginners who need a clear starting point
- Intermediate exercisers who want progressive programming
- People pursuing fat loss
- People pursuing muscle gain
- Busy people who need a prepared plan
- People who struggle with consistency or accountability
- Fitness enthusiasts who value monthly variety

The program should remain inclusive across experience levels and should not
assume access to a particular gym or equipment setup.

## What Is Included

The current offer describes:

- Monthly phase unlocks
- Strength and cardio workouts
- Structured training programs
- Nutrition guidance and awareness
- Daily habit tracking
- Progress metrics and photos
- Weekly accountability check-ins
- Community support
- App-based access

Do not promise individualized clinical care, supervised personal training, or
custom meal plans unless the operational service includes them.

## The Five-Pillar System

### Training

Progressive strength, cardio, mobility, and conditioning structure that can be
used at home, in a gym, or while traveling.

### Nutrition

Practical guidance and awareness rather than rigid or medically prescriptive
meal plans.

### Habits

Tracking and support for hydration, sleep, movement, recovery, and routine.

### Accountability

Weekly structure, check-ins, and progress tracking intended to support
consistent follow-through.

### Community

Shared support and messaging intended to make the wellness process less
isolated.

## Customer Journey

The intended journey is:

1. Review the program and pricing.
2. Join the membership.
3. Receive app access.
4. Set goals and select a program path.
5. Follow daily training, nutrition, and habit targets.
6. Track progress and complete check-ins.
7. Continue through monthly programming phases.

The current website does not implement payment, membership creation, app
provisioning, or onboarding automation.

## Primary Calls to Action

Use clear calls to action such as:

- Start Your Program
- Explore Individual Programs
- View Pricing
- Contact Us

Until checkout exists, these actions may lead to an honest inquiry or waitlist
flow. Do not imply instant access if access is not automated.

## Claims and Expectations

Approved themes:

- Build sustainable progress
- Improve consistency
- Follow a clearer plan
- Track fitness and wellness habits
- Stay organized and accountable

Avoid guaranteed weight-loss claims, guaranteed muscle gain or timelines,
medical claims, fake testimonials, fake client results, overhyped fitness
language, or claims that the program is appropriate for every person.

Include or link to a medical disclaimer where appropriate. Users should be
encouraged to consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning a
new fitness or nutrition program.
