Build a modern, mobile-first marketing website for Momentum Wellness that sells a $14.99/month subscription that unlocks all programs.

Brand:
- Name: Momentum Wellness
- Colors: Navy blue (#0B1F3B) and White (#FFFFFF). Use navy as primary background/accents, white for text/background sections. Minimal, premium look.
- Style: clean, athletic, high-trust, not “spammy.” Plenty of whitespace, strong typography, subtle shadows, rounded corners.

Primary goal:
- Convert visitors into paying subscribers for $14.99/month.
- Every page should have a clear CTA: “Start Membership” that links to a placeholder URL: https://example.com/checkout (make it easy to swap later).

Pages / routes:
1) Home (/)
2) Programs (/programs)
3) Pricing (/pricing)
4) FAQ (/faq)
5) Contact (/contact)
6) Legal (/legal) with placeholders for Terms + Privacy

Site-wide requirements:
- Sticky top nav with logo text “Momentum Wellness” and links to pages. Right side has primary button “Start Membership”.
- Responsive design (mobile first).
- SEO basics: unique title + meta description per page, semantic headings, accessible buttons/contrast.
- Footer with: © current year Momentum Wellness, links to Legal, Contact, and a short disclaimer: “Not medical advice.”

D) How it works (3 steps):

PRICING PAGE (/pricing):

CONTACT PAGE (/contact):
- Simple contact form (name, email, message) with client-side validation.

LEGAL PAGE (/legal):
- Sections for Terms of Service and Privacy Policy with placeholder text and a note: “Replace with finalized legal language.”

Technical:
- Use Next.js + React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS.
- No external paid services required.
- Include simple analytics-ready structure (but don’t add tracking code).
